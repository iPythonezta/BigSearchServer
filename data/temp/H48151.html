Fiza Junejo (Urdu: فضا جونیجو) is a Pakistani politician who served as a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan from 2002 to 2013.

Early life

[edit]

She was born to the former Prime Minister of Pakistan Muhammad Khan Junejo.[1]

Political career

[edit]

She was elected to the National Assembly of Pakistan as a candidate of Pakistan Muslim League (Q) on a seat reserved for women from Sindh in the 2008 Pakistani general election.[2][3][4]
